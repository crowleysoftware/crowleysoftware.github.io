Expert Power Platform Development

Crowley Software specializes in Microsoft Power Platform development. Whether you need to augment your power-user talent or your needs have extended into pro-developer territory, we are here to help. With years of experience in the Microsoft developer ecosystem, your project is in good hands.

I have been Microsoft certified for Power Platform Fundamentals which measures understanding of the capabilities of the Power Platform, automating basic business processes with Power Automate, performing basic data analysis with Power BI, acting more effectively by creating simple Power Apps experiences, and creating powerful chatbots by using Power Virtual Agents.

As a Microsoft Certified Azure Developer, I am well positioned to architect your entire solution, bringing together the PowerPlatform with external and custom systems.

Power Apps

A low code custom application platform. Get apps built quickly. Leverage data and systems like email, spreadsheets and databases that you already use.

Power Automate

Power Automate can execute commands without user intervention. Basically an executable flow chart, Flows wait for a trigger event and then go to work. You can perform actions when an email is received, or tweet, or hundreds of other things. You can also create approval scenarios where the Flow pauses for human approval and then continues.

Power Virtual Agents

Power Virtual Agents provide chat bots that service your user questions without the need for a live agent. A solution like this previously required data science and artificial intelligence experts. PVA brings AI to the citizen developer.

Power BI

Got data? Power BI (Business Intelligence) brings together your data sources to provide clear insights that your business needs. An intuitive interface allows power users to create powerful visualizations with dynamic capabilities.

Microsoft Teams

The Power Platform can integrate with Microsoft Teams to give your users a common, familiar place to get things done. Whether it is a custom app or a custom channel tab, your users can stay where they are already working and collaborating.
